Seating Chart Cheat Sheet

Navigation & Modes

  • Standard View: Tidy, view-only mode for daily presentation and taking attendance. Prevents accidental edits.
  • ✏️ Edit Students: Drag and drop students into seats, add/remove students, or lock specific seat assignments. Click Done Editing when finished.
  • 📐 Edit Layout: Add, move, rotate, or delete desks and tables to match your classroom configuration. Click Done Editing when finished.

Quick Tips

  • Click Done Editing anytime to secure your chart and lock in your updates.
  • Use Export PDF to download a clean, high-resolution copy for print.
